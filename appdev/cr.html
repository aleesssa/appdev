CONCLUSION

Application development is essential to various industries in today's tech-driven world. With the rapid evolution of technology, the application development industry will continue to grow to help a wide range of people with different needs and objectives. However, the integration of application development in industries comes with a number of risks. Therefore, we must consider various factors when developing an application for a project such as security and costs.
